LE labo

The “cult of imperfection" guides Le Labo’s creative spirit leading to their delicately chosen and beautifully structured scents. The creative process adheres to the Japanese world view of wabi-sabi, described as “the beauty of imperfection, the awareness of the impermanence of things. It is more of a feeling, a visual and almost spiritual impression, than a rational concept. That is the quiet beauty they try to achieve in our perfumes.

 

Fragrance

Born in Grasse and raised in New York where Le Labo’s first boutique was opened in 2006, the brand relies on its craftwomen and craftmen, the lab technician, the candle pourer, the rose picker among others, to shape and contribute to its world. Some of Le Labo locations are designed as a fragrance lab open to public where visitors have the opportunity to awaken their sense of smell to different fragrances and the raw materials used in them.
